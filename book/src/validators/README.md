# Validator Guide

Validators are the backbone of the ModernTensor network. They verify transactions, execute AI tasks, and secure the ledger.

## Requirements

- **Minimum Stake**: 10,000 MDT
- **Hardware**: 4 vCPU, 8GB RAM, 100GB SSD
- **Uptime**: >99.9%

## Rewards

Validators earn MDT from:

1. **Block Rewards**: Inflationary rewards for proposing blocks.
2. **Transaction Fees**: Gas fees from standard transactions.
3. **AI Compute Fees**: 1% protocol fee from AI tasks.
